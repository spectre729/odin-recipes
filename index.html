Bian Rou

Description

Bian rou is a simple dish from my ancestor's home: Fuzhou. It is similiar to a wonton, but made with an ultra-thin wrapper and a small ball of pork meat. It is served in a light and savory broth.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Shrimp(optional)
  • Egg
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Sha Cha Sauce
  • Fuzhou Bian Rou Wrapper

Seasonings for soup

  • Salt
  • White Pepper
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Vinegar
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Scallion
