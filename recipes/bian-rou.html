Bian Rou

Description

Bian rou is a simple dish from my ancestor's home: Fuzhou. It is similiar to a wonton, but made with an ultra-thin wrapper and a small ball of pork meat. It is served in a light and savory broth.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Shrimp(optional)
  • Egg
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Sha Cha Sauce
  • MSG
  • Fuzhou Bian Rou Wrapper

Seasonings for soup

  • Salt
  • White Pepper
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Vinegar
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Scallion
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Combine your pork, shrimp, sesame oil, soy sauce, dark soy sauce salt, chicken buoillon, sha cha sauce, and MSG into a meat mixture
  2. To assemble the bian rou, place a small ball of the meat mixture and place it in a wrapper and fold it close in any way you desire. Repeat for as much bian rou you want.
  3. In a pot, bring the water to a boil, then place the bian rou into the water. The bian rou is ready after 1-2 minutes after the water reboils.
  4. In a bowl, combine the soup seasonings. Pour hot broth to bring it together. Serve with the bian rou and enjoy!
