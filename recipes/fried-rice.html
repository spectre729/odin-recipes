Fried Rice

Description

One of the most popular chinese takeout dishes out there, fried rice is a classic. There are so many different ways and styles of fried rice, and today I will show you my take on fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Day-old rice from the fridge
  • 2 Eggs
  • Your Protein of choice
  • Half of an onion
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • MSG
  • (Optional) other veggies like carrots and peas

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. In a hot wok, fry your eggs. If needed, fry your protein
  3. Add your veggies and rice to your wok and continue frying
  4. When it is almost done, add your seasonings, MSG, soy sauce
  5. Dish up and add scallions for garnish
